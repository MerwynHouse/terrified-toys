## Where We Ship

- **New Zealand** — domestic, all regions.
- **Australia** — international from NZ.
- We don't currently ship outside NZ/AU. If that changes, this policy will be updated first.

## Founding-Buyer Run: Honest Timeline Expectations

Because the first 25 units are a genuine small-batch pre-order (not stock sitting in a warehouse), shipping happens in two stages and we'd rather be upfront about that than imply faster delivery than is realistic:

1. **Production stage:** after the founding-buyer order window closes, the batch goes into production with our manufacturer. Lead time depends on the final manufacturer quote (see `suppliers.md`) — **we'll email you a specific estimated ship date once a manufacturer is confirmed**, rather than guessing here.
2. **Shipping stage, once stock arrives with us:**
   - **NZ domestic:** typically **2-4 business days** via courier (e.g. NZ Post/CourierPost), once your order is packed and dispatched.
   - **Australia:** typically **5-10 business days** via international courier/post, once dispatched. Customs processing can occasionally add a few extra days — this is outside our control.

We'll send a confirmation email with tracking information (where the courier provides it) once your order actually ships — not at time of payment, since payment for the founding-buyer offer secures your place in the run, not an immediate shipment (see `terms-of-service.md`).

## Customs & Duties (Australia)

For Australian orders, any customs duties, taxes, or import fees are the responsibility of the recipient and aren't included in the product price or shipping cost. In practice, low-value parcels of this kind rarely attract additional charges, but we can't guarantee that and don't control Australian customs policy.

## Address Accuracy

We ship to the address provided at checkout — please double-check it before paying. Because this is a small hand-packed batch (not an automated fulfilment center), we can't always catch or fix address errors before dispatch. If a parcel is returned due to an incorrect address, redelivery costs may apply.
