# Terrified Toys — Warm Outreach DM Scripts

For reaching out personally to the first ~10 warm buyers (friends, family, dog-owner contacts). This is a message from you to someone who knows you — not a brand broadcast. Casual, personal, dark humour, self-aware about the ask. No hard sell.

General rules:
- Use their dog's name if you know it — it does more work than any line of copy.
- Don't paste this verbatim into a group chat. One person, one message.
- If they say no, that's fine — say "no worries, I'll send you the destruction video anyway" and move on.

## Short Version (iMessage / Text)

> hey [NAME] — random one but: Trinity and I have been making these plush toys for dogs that have a terrified little face, like the toy *knows* it's about to get destroyed. we're doing a tiny first batch (25 only) before we properly launch.
>
> $30 if you're in now, $40 once it's public. no pressure at all, just thought of [DOG'S NAME] immediately. want one?

**Even shorter (for people you're very close with):**

> made [DOG'S NAME] a toy that knows it's about to die. $30 for the first 25, want one before strangers get to.

## Slightly Longer Version (Instagram DM)

> Hey [NAME]! Random DM but had to send this your way.
>
> Trinity and I have been working on something stupid for the last while — plush dog toys with a face that looks genuinely terrified, because it knows exactly what's about to happen to it. The destruction is the whole point. It's also actually a decent toy, not just a one-hit gag.
>
> We're doing a tiny first run before we launch properly — 25 units total. Since you're one of the people whose dog I actually think about when I see these (no offense to [DOG'S NAME], it's a compliment), wanted to give you first dibs.
>
> $30 NZD if you're in as a founding buyer, $40 once we open it up publicly. No pressure if it's not your thing — but if you want one, just say the word and I'll sort you out before anyone else.
>
> Also if [DOG'S NAME] destroys it on camera, I will pay you in compliments forever.

## Optional follow-up (if they go quiet after a few days)

> no rush on this btw, the 25 spots aren't going anywhere fast lol. lmk if you're keen, no worries either way

## Optional close (after they say yes)

> amazing, locking you in. I'll send payment details + we'll keep you posted on when stock lands. fair warning — I will absolutely ask you to film [DOG'S NAME]'s reaction.
