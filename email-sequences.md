# Terrified Toys — MailerLite Email Sequence Copy

Three emails per group (welcome, mid-sequence warm-up, founding-buyer conversion), tailored to why that group signed up. Build these as automations in MailerLite, triggered on joining each group — see `marketing-campaign-plan.md` for where each fits in the calendar (welcome on signup, warm-up weeks 2-4, conversion email week 7 when demo stock arrives).

General rules for all five:
- Subject lines stay short and in-voice — no exclamation points, no "🚨 LIMITED TIME 🚨" energy.
- Every email ends with the same core facts: $30 NZD founding price (vs $40 later), 25-unit cap, NZ/AU shipping.
- Keep each email under ~150 words. This is a dog-toy brand, not a SaaS onboarding flow.

---

## 1. Waiting List (general/default group)

### (a) Welcome Email
**Subject:** It knows you signed up.

> Hey — you're in.
>
> You just joined the waitlist for Terrified Toys: plush dog toys with a face that knows exactly what's about to happen to it. The destruction is the whole point. It's also a genuinely decent toy, not just a one-hit gag.
>
> We're making 25 for the first run. You're on the list for first access at $30 NZD (it's $40 once we go public) — no payment needed yet, we'll email when stock's ready.
>
> In the meantime, we'll send the odd update — design reveals, maybe a video of a dog committing a crime. Nothing else.
>
> Talk soon,
> Daniel & Trinity

### (b) Mid-Sequence Warm-Up (weeks 2-4)
**Subject:** Meet the toys

> Quick one — wanted to show you what you signed up for.
>
> Three designs so far, all with the same problem: they know what's coming. [Link to design reveal content / Instagram post]
>
> Demo stock is in production now. Still 25 units total, still $30 for waitlist members. We'll let you know the second it lands.
>
> — Daniel & Trinity

### (c) Founding-Buyer Conversion (week 7, stock arrives)
**Subject:** It's here. You're first.

> Stock just landed — and you get first dibs before this goes out to anyone else.

>
