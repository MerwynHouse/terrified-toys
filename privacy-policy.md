## What We Actually Collect

This policy is written to match what the site genuinely does right now, not a generic template:

- **Name and email address** — collected when you join the waitlist on the pre-launch page or any persona landing page. This goes to our serverless function (`api/subscribe.js`), which adds you to a MailerLite subscriber list (you'll be placed in one of five groups depending on which page you signed up through — this just determines which emails you get, it doesn't otherwise change how your data is handled).
- **Standard web hosting logs** — our site is hosted on Vercel, which automatically logs technical information (IP address, browser type, pages visited) as part of normal web server operation. We don't currently run any separate analytics or advertising tracking (no Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel) — if that changes, this policy will be updated first.
- **Payment information (once order-taking goes live)** — when we start taking orders, payment will be processed via Stripe. We will not see or store your full card details — Stripe handles that directly and provides us with order confirmation and basic transaction details only.
- We do not currently collect anything beyond this. If a future feature collects more (e.g. a contact form, a survey), this policy needs to be updated to cover it before that feature goes live.

## Why We Collect It

- To send you updates about the Terrified Toys launch, including early access to the founding-buyer offer
- To process and fulfil orders, once ordering is live
- To run the website reliably and securely (hosting logs)

We don't sell your information to anyone, and we don't share it with third parties except the service providers needed to actually run the business (MailerLite for email, Vercel for hosting, Stripe for payment once live).

## Your Rights (NZ Privacy Act 2020)

Under New Zealand's Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to:
- Ask what personal information we hold about you
- Ask us to correct it if it's wrong
- Ask us to delete it (we'll action this unless we have a legal reason to keep it, e.g. transaction records)
- Unsubscribe from emails at any time — every email includes an unsubscribe link, and MailerLite handles this automatically

To exercise any of these, email [FILL IN CONTACT EMAIL]. If you're unhappy with how we've handled your information, you can also contact the Office of the Privacy Commissioner ([privacy.org.nz](https://www.privacy.org.nz)).

**Note on overseas data storage:** MailerLite, Vercel, and Stripe all store data on servers outside New Zealand. Under the Privacy Act 2020, this is fine as long as the receiving country has comparable privacy protections or the provider is otherwise bound to protect the information appropriately — all three are established providers with their own privacy/data-protection commitments.
